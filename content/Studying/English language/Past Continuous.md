 Use Past Continuous:
 1. to talk about events that were in progress at a specific time in the past.
 2. to show that two actions were happening at the same time
S + was/were + Ving
S + was/were + not + Ving
Was/Were + S + Ving?

Special cases 
when Past Simple + Past Continuous 
When he came in, she was cooking.

Past Continuous + when/while Past Continuous 
He was cooking dinner while she was reading.
