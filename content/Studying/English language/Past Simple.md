 Past Simple
 Use Past Simple for completed actions in the past and states in the past.
 Use Past Simple with yesterday, ago, last week/month/year, in December, in 1999
To be:
S + was/were
S + was not / were not + V
Was/Were + S
Question word/phrase + was/were + S

Other verbs
S + Ved/d or V2
S + did not + V
Did + S + V
Question word/phrase + did + S + V
