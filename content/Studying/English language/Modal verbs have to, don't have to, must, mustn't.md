 Have to
 Use have to to talk about obligations which from outside the speaker
 Use had to instead of must in the past
 S + have to/has to/had to + V
 Do/Does/Did + S + have to + V?

Don't have to 
Use don't have to when it's not necessary to do something.
S + don't/doesn't/didn't + have to + V

Must
Use must:
1. to say something is necessary to do
2. for personal obligations
3. for orders and written rules

Use must only to talk about the present. 
Use mustn't when it's forbidden to do something.

S + must + V
S + must not + V
