Use the passive voice: 
1. when it's not important who does the action or you do not know who does the action (Cotton is used to make clothes)
2. when you want to focus on the action, not on the subject (In our company, cars are repaired by robots)

To be (Present Simple form) + V3/Ved
am/is/are + V3/Ved
am not / is not / are not + V3/Ved
Am/Is/Are + subject + V3/Ved
