 Use Present Perfect to talk about:
 1. finished actions with the result in the past
 2. unfinished actions (usually with markers for/since)
 3. life experience when the time is not important

Use Present Perfect with the markers just, already, yet, never, ever, for, since.

S + have/has not + V(e)d
