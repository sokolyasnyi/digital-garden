Future Simple:
1. facts about the future
2. opinions/guesses about the future
3. decisions made at the moment of speaking
4. promises

S + will + V
S + will not + V
Will + S + V?

To be going to
1. plans you made in the past
2. actions starting to happen

S + am/is/are + going to + V - Look at the sky! It is going to rain.
S + am/is/are + not + going to + V - We are not going to meet today.
Am/Is/Are + S +  + going to + V? When are they going to move?
